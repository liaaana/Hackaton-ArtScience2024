Push-up Technique:
  • Place your hands firmly on the ground directly under your shoulders.
  • Keep your body straight and slowly lower it down.
  • Pull your shoulder blades back and down, keeping your elbows close to your body.
  • Exhale as you push yourself back to the starting position.

Position the device so that you can be seen in full height. The app will track repetitions and check execution technique.

Recommended to perform 20 repetitions
