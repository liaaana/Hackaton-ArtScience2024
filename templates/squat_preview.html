Squat Technique:
  1. Place your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Bend your knees and hips, sitting back into a squat, lowering your body.
  3. Continue downward until your thighs are parallel to the floor.
  4. Return to the starting position, straightening your legs and hips.

Position the device so that you can be seen in full height. The app will track repetitions and check execution technique.

Recommended to perform 20 repetitions
